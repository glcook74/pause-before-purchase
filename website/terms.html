2. What Dopamine Delay is

Dopamine Delay is a productivity tool designed to help adults manage impulse spending by introducing a brief pause at online checkouts.

Dopamine Delay is not a medical device. It is not a substitute for professional financial advice, psychological counselling, or medical treatment for ADHD or any other condition. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition.

3. Free and Pro tiers

Free tier: The core Dopamine Delay experience is free and will remain free. This includes the pause overlay, purchase classification, dopamine alternatives (limited selection), save for later, Delay Points, dark pattern alerts, and the today dashboard view.

Pro tier: Dopamine Delay Pro offers additional features including unlimited alternatives, spending analytics, full saved items history, late-night mode, personalised profiles, priority support, and early access to the mobile app. Pro is available as a monthly subscription.
