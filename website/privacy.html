Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who we are

Dopamine Delay is a product of KailieTech Ltd, a company registered in Northern Ireland. We build tools that help adults with ADHD manage impulse spending.

Contact: hello@dopaminedelay.com

2. What data we collect

In Phase 1, all data generated by the Dopamine Delay Chrome extension — including pause history, Delay Points, saved items, streaks, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage system.

We do not collect, transmit, or store this data on our servers. We have no access to your browsing activity, purchase history, or any data stored by the extension.

3. Waitlist emails

If you join our mobile app waitlist, your email address is collected via Formspree (a third-party form service). This email will be used solely to notify you when the mobile app is available. We will not sell, share, or use your email for any other purpose.

4. Stripe payments

If you subscribe to Dopamine Delay Pro, payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. We receive only confirmation of payment status — we never see, store, or process your card details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to all payment data.

5. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies. We do not use analytics services that track individual users. We do not serve advertisements.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR and UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

Since the extension stores data locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can clear extension data through Chrome's settings.

For waitlist emails or payment-related requests, contact us at hello@dopaminedelay.com and we'll respond within 30 days.

7. Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "last updated" date above. For material changes that affect how your data is handled, we will notify users through the extension or by email where possible.
